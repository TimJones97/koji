Episode #1

Mariano Conti

27th June, 2021

 | 

55 minutes

Share

LinkedInTelegramTwitterYoutube

Uncertainty. Inflation. Corruption. How might you combat such existential challenges? Purchasing US dollars on the blackmarket might be one option. Purchasing physical assets immediately, to capture the maximum value at a given time, another. These challenges were very real for Argentines. One Argentinian was awoken to the potential of a new technology, and made the decision to be part of the solution. In this episode, we learn about Mariano Conti’s journey. He relives the events that shaped his mindset, making it clear why he became involved with MakerDAO: a decentralised autonomous organisation which maintains and regulates DAI, a crypto stablecoin. His passions, fears, sacrifices and future ambitions are brought to the forefront. Mariano Conti tells his story in High Definition.
